What we offer...


Here at the food computing academy we offer a variety of services. We specialise in providing education to everyone regarding food safety, food microbiology research and education worldwide. We, as an organisation publish events, research and news everything food safety, biology and service related! Any inquiries - refer to the contact us page and we are excited to help provide education worldwide.


At the Food Computing Academy, our programs are designed and led by researchers and industry professionals who are passionate about bridging the gap between science and everyday understanding. Whether you're a student, educator, or simply curious about what's on your plate - there's a place for you here.

What is FCA?

Food Computing Academy (FCA) is a specialised accelerator that helps food and beverage SMEs adopt data-driven, technology-enabled ways of working.

We bridge the gap between traditional food production and modern digital transformation by combining food science, AI, and practical manufacturing expertise.

— Food Computing Academy

The Problems We Solve

  • Digital and AI tools

  • Operational knowledge is often siloed
